5 Expenses Sole Traders Often Miss

Are you claiming everything you are entitled to? Many self-employed people miss legitimate costs that could reduce their tax bill significantly.

Common missed expenses include: home office costs, travel between workplaces, tools and equipment, mobile and internet, and professional subscriptions. Even small amounts add up — claiming correctly can save you hundreds or even thousands of pounds each year.

When Should You Become a Limited Company?

Changing your business structure can save tax, but it is not right for everyone. Here is how to decide the best time to make the switch.

Typically, once profits exceed £30,000–£40,000 per year, a limited company often becomes more tax-efficient. However, factors like liability protection, future plans and administrative burden all matter — we help you weigh everything up clearly.

Key Tips for Property Landlords

Understand finance cost relief, allowable repairs and how to structure your property portfolio to be as tax-efficient as possible.

Remember that interest relief is restricted for higher-rate taxpayers, but there are still ways to plan. Keep detailed records of repairs vs improvements, and consider ownership structures carefully — getting this right makes a big difference to your net return.
